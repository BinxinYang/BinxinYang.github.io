Binxin Yang

I obtained a Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I was supervised by Professors Xiaoyan Sun and Xuejin Chen. Prior to that, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China in 2019. Since July 2024, I have been working as a Computer Vision Algorithm Researcher at Tencent WeChat Business Group. My research focuses on video generation and editing, as well as image generation and editing.
